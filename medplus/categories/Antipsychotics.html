Antipsychotics

Antipsychotics are medicines that are mainly used to treat thought and cognitive disorders like schizophrenia and sociopathic behaviour. They are used to treat mania caused by bipolar disorder. They may also be used to treat severe depression, severe agitation and severe anxiety.
